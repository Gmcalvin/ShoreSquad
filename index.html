About ShoreSquad

Why ShoreSquad?

Singapore's beaches are facing unprecedented pollution. ShoreSquad empowers Gen-Z to take action with fun, organized cleanup events. We believe environmental action should be social, trackable, and rewarding.

Our Mission

To mobilize youth across Singapore for regular beach cleanups, building a community that cares about our coasts while having fun doing it.
